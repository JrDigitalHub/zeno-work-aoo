# Zeno-Work: The Autonomous Operations Officer (AOO)

## 🧠 Sovereign Business Intelligence
Zeno-Work is a specialized Neural Operating System designed to automate the growth and operational lifecycle of modern SMEs. It is built to replace fragmented SaaS tools with a unified, autonomous reasoning engine.

### 🛠 The Triple-Threat Architecture
- **THE PREDATOR:** An active growth module that scrapes high-intent market signals and executes autonomous lead-generation and outreach.
- **THE SENTINEL:** An event-driven back-office engine that orchestrates complex workflows, inventory, and financial tracking.
- **THE ORACLE:** A proactive consulting layer that utilizes a Neo4j Knowledge Graph to provide strategic business insights and risk detection.

### 💻 Technical Stack
- **Backend:** Go (Golang) for high-performance concurrent task orchestration.
- **Intelligence:** Local Small Language Models (SLM) for deterministic reasoning (No-Wrapper Mandate).
- **Memory:** Qdrant (Vector) + Neo4j (Graph) for long-term operational context.
- **Comms:** Sovereign Voice Engine via Whisper/StyleTTS2.
